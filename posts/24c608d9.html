Privacy Policy

Created2023-07-01|Updated2023-07-01
|Post View:

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how gerryleng.com (the “Website”) collects, uses, and safeguards certain information when you visit or interact with the Website.

Information Collection:

1.1 Personal Information:
While gerryleng.com is primarily a static website, there are certain instances where we may collect and store personal information voluntarily provided by users. This information may include your name, email address, or other contact details when you submit a contact form or reach out to us via email. We will only collect and use this personal information for the specific purpose for which it was provided.

1.2 Log Files and Analytics:
Like many websites, gerryleng.com collects certain non-personal information automatically. This information may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and other usage details. We use this information to analyze trends, administer the Website, track user movement, and gather demographic information for internal purposes. This data is collected using analytics services and does not personally identify you.

Use of Information:

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries or provide requested information.
  • To improve the content, functionality, and user experience of the Website.
  • To send you periodic emails or newsletters, if you have opted-in to receive such communications.
  • To analyze and track usage patterns on the Website for statistical purposes.
  • To enforce our Terms and Conditions and protect the rights, property, and safety of gerryleng.com and its users.

Data Sharing and Disclosure:

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may engage trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Website or providing services to you. These service providers have access to the information necessary to perform their functions on our behalf and are obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as a court order or government investigation.
    Data Security:
    We take reasonable measures to protect the security of your information and prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Third-Party Links:

The Website may contain links to third-party websites. We have no control over the content, privacy practices, or security of these external sites. Therefore, we cannot be held responsible for the protection and privacy of any information you provide while visiting such sites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of those third-party websites before providing any personal information.

Children’s Privacy:

The Website is not directed toward individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that we may have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately, and we will take appropriate steps to remove the information.

Your Rights:

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, update, correct, or delete your information. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 8.

Contact Information:

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the data practices of gerryleng.com, please contact us at [insert contact information].
