S1 (7/17/23)

Rain

Do you like rainy days?

  1. No, there are several reasons: one is because rainy days can somehow affect my emotion and make me feel sullen. Consecuently, I find myself hard to step out of my “confort zone” than it does not rain. The other thing is that we get to bring an umbrella to avoid getting wet, However, I have to confess I prefer not to bring extra stuff even if I dreach.
  2. No, there are several reasons why rainy days affect me negatively. Firstly, the gloomy weather tends to impact my emotions and make me feel sullen. Consequently, it becomes challenging for me to step out of my comfort zone compared to non-rainy days. Secondly, although carrying an umbrella helps avoid getting wet, I must confess that I prefer not to bring extra items, even if it means getting drenched.

What do you do on rainy days?

  1. I never use the weather forecast as a reference for my schedule, even if weather is extrem, I seldom changes my plan. What I do change is the way in order to perform my plan. For instance, If I originally want to go to a supermarket to buy some milk by walk, I will go by car instead.
  2. I never use the weather forecast as a reference for my schedule, even in extreme weather conditions. While I seldom change my plans, I do adapt the way I execute them. For example, if I initially intended to walk to the supermarket to buy some milk, I would opt to go by car instead.

Do you prefer rainy or sunny days?

  1. Of course sunny days. Just like I mentioned earlier, I can not gain any good from rain both physically or mentally. There are many drawbacks of downpour in one form or another, for instance, it can let people drenched, cause traffic jam, road collapse and things like that. As an addition, it weary me and let me feel unpleasent from my own experience.

How does rain affect your like in your country?

  1. In most cases, when raindall descends upon the city, it has the effect of making the streets and public spaces less crowded, as people seek shelter indoors or for alternative transportion, but you’re likely to hear the incessant BEEP-BEEP of cars and HONK-HONK of buses in residential areas.

Day off

What do you usually do when you have days off?

  1. I used to very often, go out and play catcher with my friend. One of us would hit the ball with a bat and the other one would catch it. However, when exams are approaching, we allocate additional time to focus on improving our grades. Nervertheless, I always strive to strike a balance between my studing and my daily life.

Do you usually spend your days off with your parents or with your friends?

  1. My days off are spend with my friends as I moverd to the UK for my study while my parents work in China, However, when I was young

When was the last time you had a few days off?

  1. A few days ago. I went a walk around an abbey near my boarding house.

What would you like to do if you had a day off tomorrow?

  1. Let me see… I actually have not made my mind yet, but I guess I would prefer reading the book that I have not yet finshed because I have been preparing this ielts exams ever since last month, I barely have my own spare time to do anything extra, so I can’t wait to read it.

Bags

Do you usually carry a bag?

  1. Nornally, I prefer not to carry extra stuff, beacuse if I have many bags I need to consider which one is the best fit in different occasions, which is time consuming to try to figure out which one is the best fit. Therefore, if I have nothing or only a few things to bring, I rather carry them with my hands.

What kinds of bags would you use when traveling?

  1. A backpack is needed whenever the number of items or weight gets large as it is big in size and light in weight. Additionaly, my backpack is water-proof, so I don’t necessarily need to bother it gets wet. Howerver if it is a trip away from home, I will propably use a suitcase instead where I can put my personal blongings and cloths.

Do you change your bags often?

  1. I only have a suitcase along with a backpack for now, and I have not changed them for 2 years, they are clearly high-quality and durable. Functionally like new except they are a little faded, I think they’ll last another 5-6 years.
